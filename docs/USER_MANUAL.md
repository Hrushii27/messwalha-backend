# MessWalha User Manual

## For Students
### Finding a Mess
- Use the **Find Mess** page to search by name or location.
- Filter results based on price, rating, or food type.
- Click on a mess to view its profile, photos, and current menu.

### Subscribing
- Select a subscription plan (Daily, Weekly, Monthly) on the mess details page.
- Complete the payment process.
- Your subscription will appear in your **Student Dashboard**.

### Giving Feedback
- After using a mess, you can leave a review and a star rating from the mess page.

## For Mess Owners
### Managing Your Mess
- List your mess by providing description, location, and photos.
- Update your **Daily Menu** so students can see what's being served today.
- View and manage your current subscribers from the **Owner Dashboard**.

## For Administrators
### System Overview
- Monitor total users and active subscriptions from the **Admin Dashboard**.
- Verify new mess listings before they go live.
- Manage system health and review logs.
